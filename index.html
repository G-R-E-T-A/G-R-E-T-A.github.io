G.R.E.T.A

Abstract

        In India, about 12.3 million people are hearing impaired (moderateor complete). Indian Sign Language (ISL) which is developed specifically forIndians is taught in over 850 schools where few teachers have the task oftraining large class. Alot of research has been done in the corresponding field of American SignLanguage(ASL), but unfortunately the same cannot be said for ISL therefore what we introduce G.R.E.T.A (Gesture Recognition Endemic Teaching Aid). Lackof standard datasets, occluded features and variation in the language withlocality have been the major barriers which has led to little research beingdone in ISL.
